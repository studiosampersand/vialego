What happens with my data?

MoBud stores your mobility data locally on this device. Google Drive is optional and uses your own app data folder. Local and Drive data are merged where possible; MoBud does not sell data and does not use a central trip database.

Automatic sync runs when MoBud opens, after relevant changes and periodically while the app is active. Manual backups are separate recovery points.

Google Drive is optional. MoBud remains local-first. Your backup stays in your Google account. You manage storage accumulation and retention.

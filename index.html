Three real use cases

Concrete administration, without the spreadsheet chaos.

1. Motorcycle & taxes

Track purchases, maintenance, repairs and kilometres. Export clear monthly and annual reports for your accountant or tax administration while keeping the original documents yourself.

2. Commute benefit

See exactly how many days you cycled to work versus used a private vehicle. Compare kilometres, costs and the estimated financial advantage over time.

3. Fleet efficiency

Compare every vehicle in your garage or fleet — bicycle, car, motorcycle or even helicopter — and see what is used most and what delivers the best value.
